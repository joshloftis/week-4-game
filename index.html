A Game of Crystals

Game instructions:

You've been given a random number, which resets for each game.

There are four crystals. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. The number of points for each crystal is chosen randomly and resets for each game. You will have to remember the value for each crystal if you want to win the game.

To the win the game, you must match your score to the random number that has been given to you. The game does not end until you match the score or exceed it. If you exceed the number, you lose.

Good luck. Click a crytal to get started.
